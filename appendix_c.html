space_heat_effy(_Q_space)[source]¶

Calculate the space heating efficiency.

Efficiencies work a bit differently for community systems - system efficiency is not accounted to in calculating energy consumption and cost (so we return 100% here, scaled for additional loss factors. System effy is included in CO2 and primary energy factors.

Parameters:_Q_space – ignored, included for compatiblity with equivalent function for regular heating
